About Me

I’m Yenma Abambire Bawa, a driven and versatile junior Computer Science student at Ashesi University, Ghana, with a strong passion for blending technology, creativity, and innovation. I specialize in full-stack web development, data structures, and software engineering, with experience in languages such as JavaScript, PHP, HTML, CSS, Python, and R. Beyond my technical expertise, I am a writer, poet, and entrepreneur, dedicated to creating impactful solutions and meaningful art. I am the founder of Zenforia, an aesthetics brand focused on candle-making and curating serene environments, and I am currently developing Unwind, a mental health wellness app aimed at promoting student well-being. Additionally, I’m working on launching Eunoia, a care and gift package business rooted in self-care and empowerment. My projects reflect my commitment to merging technical skills with real-world applications, whether it's developing platforms like Ayera, a lost-and-found web platform for students, or engaging in mental health research on the effectiveness of wellness apps in reducing stress. I believe in continuous growth, leadership, and using both my analytical and creative strengths to leave a positive mark in every space I occupy.
